## Program Description

Hermes SMS Assistant is a personal AI assistant service operated by John Horn.
Users text the service number to receive automated informational responses,
including reminders, weather information, scheduling assistance, and answers
to general questions. All messages are user-initiated and opt-in based.

## Consent and Opt-In

By sending an initial text message to the service number, you consent to
receive automated text messages from Hermes SMS Assistant in response to your
inquiries. No messages are sent to you unless you text the service first.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your use of the service. You control when
messages are sent by texting the service number.

## Fees

Message and data rates may apply according to your wireless carrier plan.

## Opt-Out

Reply **STOP** at any time to opt out of receiving messages. You will receive
one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
Reply **START** to opt back in.

## Help and Support
