# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

## Information We Collect

We collect the phone numbers of users who voluntarily text our SMS service to receive automated responses from an AI assistant.

## How We Use Your Information

- To send automated responses to your text messages
- To maintain conversation context for ongoing assistance
- We do **not** sell, share, or distribute phone numbers to third parties

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on user-initiated conversations. You control when messages are sent by texting the service.

## Opt-Out

Reply **STOP** at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply **START** to resume.

## Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply based on your wireless carrier plan.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, text the service number directly.
